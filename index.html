Why I code

Sometimes people ask me; Why do I code? Is it because im a nerd who likes geeking out on computer parts, or is it for the challenge because i just love solving little maths problems in my spare time? Or is it for the money? After all, coding is just a job isnt it... You go in, put in your 9 - 5, clock in your hours. Not Quite...

Code, its not just a job.. It is a way of life, it will take you places and show you things. Show you a different way of looking at the world. Code took me to the corners of the world with nothing but a laptop in hand, I would work on apps and websites from anywhere with an internet connection.

Coding is self expression, in a digital world I can live in a world that, I design. Customization, Automation, Tooling, UI, Design and Optimisation. Anything I dreamed up I could build, No labratory needed, no funding, no employees, no approval necessary from anybody. Just give me a laptop and a dream. So what are you waiting for?
